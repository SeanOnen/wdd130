Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa, bordered by Kenya, Sudan, Rwanda, the Democratic Republic of Congo, and Tanzania. It has a diverse geography that includes the Nile basin, volcanic mountains, and the rainforests of the central plateau. As of 2024, Uganda has a population of nearly 46 million, with Kampala as its capital city.
